What is TinkerHub?

Peer Learning Community

TinkerHub is a community of tinkerers, makers, and learners spread across 300+ campuses in India. We believe in the power of learning together.

Learn by Doing

Through study jams, hackathons, and hands-on workshops, members learn real-world skills, build projects, and grow their tech careers.

Campus Chapters

Each campus chapter is led by a core team of passionate students who organise events, mentor peers, and build a thriving tech culture on campus.
